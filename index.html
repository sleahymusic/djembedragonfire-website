The Story

Passion, playfulness, and serious musicianship.

Music has been part of my life since childhood. Classically trained from a young age, I have spent years exploring the many ways a voice can tell a story, from the intimacy of a jazz standard to the emotional honesty of a contemporary ballad.

I do not believe in staying inside a single genre. Some nights call for Broadway. Others need blues, pop, country, holiday favorites, or songs that simply make us laugh.

The goal is always the same: to create moments where people feel uplifted, connected, comforted, inspired, and reminded that music can still move us.

Invited into Second Life performance by the late Crito Galtier, Djembe continues that spark with gratitude, heart, and a little playful fire.
